hello-spec · explained without the jargon

Teaching computers to check code safely

A small, working demo that combines two open security standards — and shows how to make an AI prove its work before anyone trusts it.
🏠 Like a building inspection: the building code is the rulebook of what's safe; a trustworthy inspector never guesses, always shows evidence, gets a second opinion, and leaves the owner in charge; hello-spec is the model house we walk through to see it work.
